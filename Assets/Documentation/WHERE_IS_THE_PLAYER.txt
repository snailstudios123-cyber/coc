Q: Why don't I see the Player in Hierarchy?
A: You only see it in PLAY MODE! It spawns at runtime.

Q: Where is the Player prefab?
A: Assets/Resources/Player.prefab

Q: How do I change where Player spawns?
A: Move the PlayerSpawnPoint in Bedroom, or edit spawn positions in SceneTransitionTrigger components.

Q: Can I add Player to scene manually?
A: NO! It will conflict with the singleton. Player auto-spawns.

Q: Player spawns in wrong location?
A: Check SceneTransitionTrigger "Player Spawn Position" field on the trigger you walked through.

Q: Player disappears after scene change?
A: Check that PlayerController has DontDestroyOnLoad. Check Console for errors.

Q: How do I test spawn positions?
A: Enter Play mode, use Scene view to see where player appears, adjust spawn positions in triggers.
