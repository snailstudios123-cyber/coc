OVERVIEW:
---------
This spell charges while holding X, draining mana. When you release X, 
it unleashes rapid fire sword strikes that deal massive damage!

The longer you charge (up to 3 seconds), the more strikes you get (max 5).

        Condition: FireSwordStrike trigger
        Exit Time: unchecked
        Transition Duration: 0

HOW IT WORKS:
=============

1. HOLD X KEY:
   ‚Ä¢ Starts charging (plays charge animation)
   ‚Ä¢ Drains mana continuously
   ‚Ä¢ Player cannot move while charging
   ‚Ä¢ Charge up to 3 seconds max

2. RELEASE X KEY:
   ‚Ä¢ Performs rapid fire sword strikes
   ‚Ä¢ Number of strikes depends on charge time:
     - 0.2s charge = 1 strike
     - 0.6s charge = 2 strikes
     - 1.5s charge = 3 strikes
     - 2.4s charge = 4 strikes
     - 3.0s charge = 5 strikes (max)
   
   ‚Ä¢ Each strike:
     ‚úì Hits all enemies in attack area
     ‚úì Deals 15 damage per hit
     ‚úì Spawns fire slash effect
     ‚úì Gives back 0.05 mana per enemy hit
     ‚úì Happens every 0.1 seconds

3. AUTO-CANCEL:
   ‚Ä¢ If you run out of mana while charging, it cancels
   ‚Ä¢ If you charge for less than 0.2 seconds, nothing happens

=====
‚Ä¢ Adjust "damagePerStrike" for balance (15 damage √ó 5 strikes = 75 total!)
‚Ä¢ Lower "timeBetweenStrikes" (e.g., 0.05) for even faster attacks
‚Ä¢ Increase "manaPerSecond" to make it cost more mana to charge
‚Ä¢ Adjust "attackArea" size to match your sword animation reach


TROUBLESHOOTING:

   ‚Ä¢ Check that you have enough mana
   ‚Ä¢ Verify Attack Point is assigned

   ‚Ä¢ Make sure animation clips are assigned to states

Q: Strikes don't hit enemies?
A: ‚Ä¢ Check "Enemy Layer" is set to "Attackable"
   ‚Ä¢ Adjust "Attack Area" size
   ‚Ä¢ Verify Attack Point position in scene

   ‚Ä¢ Find: Input.GetKey(KeyCode.X)
